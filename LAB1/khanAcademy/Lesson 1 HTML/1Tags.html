What is Afrobeats

Afrobeats (plural) is a modern, popular music genre from West Africa—primarily Nigeria and Ghana—that
traditional African rhythms, highlife, and percussion with contemporary electronic, hip-hop,
and dancehall influences. It features 4/4 beats, often incorporating Pidgin English,
local slangs, and English lyrics, popularized by artists like Wizkid, Burna Boy, and Davido.

Popular Songs

Popular Afrobeat songs dominating global charts include Rema’s "Calm Down" (59 weeks at #1)
Tyla’s "Water" (55 weeks at #1), and Burna Boy’s hits like "Last Last" and "Ye".
Other major hits frequently played include "Essence" (Wizkid ft. Tems), "Rush" (Ayra Starr),
"Lonely At The Top" (Asake), and "Soweto" (Victony).
