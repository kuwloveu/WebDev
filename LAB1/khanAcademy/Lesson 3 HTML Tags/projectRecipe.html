Beshbarmak

  • Time: 3 hours
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Meat (Lamb or Beef) 1.5 kg
Flour 500 g
Eggs 2
Onions 3-4 large ones

Step 1: Boil the meat in a large pot for about 2-3 hours until tender. Skim the foam constantly.

Step 2: Prepare the dough by mixing flour, eggs, and a bit of broth. Roll it thin and cut into large squares.

Step 3: Cook the dough squares in the meat broth for a few minutes.

Step 4: Slice the onions and simmer them in a small pot with the top fatty layer of the broth and black pepper.

Step 5: Place the dough on a large plate, put the meat on top, and pour the onion sauce over everything.

Source: mind of Ramazan
